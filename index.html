Monkey D. Luffy

About Luffy

A young man known for his trademark straw hat and captain of the Straw Hat pirates on a journey to become the King of the Pirates to conquer the seas and find the legendary treasure, One Piece. His body is also made of rubber due to eating the gum-gum fruit.

Luffy's characteristics

  1. He will do anything to stand up for his comrades
  2. He is incredibly optimstic
  3. He has a way of turning certain enemies into friends
